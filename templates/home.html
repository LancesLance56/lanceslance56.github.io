Historical Timeline

900s - Pre-Colonial

Early Trade

Long before Spanish colonization, Chinese merchants were already trading ceramics and silk with the natives of the Philippines.

1594 - Binondo Established

The Parian

The Spanish Governor-General established Binondo as a permanent settlement for Catholic Chinese, creating the world's oldest Chinatown.

1900s - Integration

Rise of Tsinoy

Chinese schools and newspapers flourished. The term "Tsinoy" began to emerge as a distinct identity blending both cultures.

Present Day

Modern Identity

Today, the Filipino-Chinese community is a pillar of the economy and culture, celebrating a unique dual heritage.
